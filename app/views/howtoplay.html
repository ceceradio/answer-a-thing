How to play

  1. Each round, one player is chosen to be the caller.
  2. The caller picks a question to ask.
  3. Then, everyone else writes or draws an answer to that question.
  4. Once everyone has submitted an answer, the caller secretly picks which answer they like best.
  5. Everyone else then bets up to 2 times on which answer they think the caller picked.
  6. You get one point for each time you bet correctly, and 1 point for having your answer chosen.
  7. The game normally ends after everyone has played 2 or 3 turns as a caller, but you can keep playing for as long as you like!
